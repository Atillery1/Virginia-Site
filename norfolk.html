Discover Norfolk

Norfolk, incorporated in 1736, is home to 245,000 residents and is known for its bustling port, naval history, and waterfront culture. As the hub of the Hampton Roads region, Norfolk offers a vibrant mix of old and new, blending maritime heritage with contemporary living.
