Discover Richmond

Richmond, the capital of Virginia, is a vibrant city that blends its rich history with modern-day charm. With a population of 226,000, this historic city is known for its pivotal role in the Civil War, stunning architecture, and thriving arts and food scenes.

Highlights

History: Explore Civil War landmarks and museums that bring Richmond's past to life.

Arts & Culture: From murals to galleries, Richmond is a haven for art enthusiasts.

James River: Adventure awaits with kayaking, hiking, and picturesque views along the river.

Quick Facts

  • Population: 226,000
  • Year Incorporated: 1742
  • Region: Central Virginia
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $47,250
